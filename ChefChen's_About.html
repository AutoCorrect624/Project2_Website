About Us

Chef Chen's Express offers delicious dining, takeout and delivery to Harrisburg, PA. Chef Chen's Express is a cornerstone in the Harrisburg community and has been recognized for its outstanding Chinese cuisine, excellent service and friendly staff. Our Chinese restaurant is known for its modern interpretation of classic dishes and its insistence on only using high quality fresh ingredients.
