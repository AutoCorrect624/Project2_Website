About Us

Santa Fe Taco Factory is bringing traditional Latin Cuisine right to the heart of Harrisburg. We are located on the 2nd floor of Strawberry Square right in the heart of Downtown Harrisburg. We have the most delicious tacos burritos salads and even Rotisserie Chicken with traditional American sides for the perfect take home meal for the family.
